WEPPCAT is an interactive, iterative tool used for helping to understand and assess management practice (BMP) effectiveness under user-defined climate change scenarios. The purpose of this product is to provide interested users an online tool that can be used for assessing and managing the impacts of climate change on erosion rates for soils under agricultural management in the United States. WEPPCAT utilizes the capabilities of the WEPP model, in conjunction with climate generation tools CLIGEN (USDA-ARS) and RockCLIME (USDA-FS). It also includes a new capability to independently alter the intensity of rainfall events for any location's climate in the nation.
